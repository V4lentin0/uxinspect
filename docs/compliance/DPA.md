**Processor:** [uxinspect legal entity, address, jurisdiction]

## 2. Subject Matter

This Addendum supplements the Master Services Agreement between the
parties. It governs the processing of Personal Data submitted to the
uxinspect service by the Controller or on the Controller's behalf.

## 3. Duration

This Addendum applies for the duration of the MSA and any period
thereafter during which Processor retains Personal Data.

## 4. Nature and Purpose of Processing

Processor operates an automated UI/UX testing service that records
browser interactions against Controller-specified URLs. Personal Data
is processed to the extent it appears in the Controller's own test
fixtures, captured screenshots, or request/response payloads.

## 5. Categories of Data Subjects

- Controller's employees running the service
- End users whose test fixtures include identifiers (only if the
  Controller chooses to exercise real user data in tests)

## 6. Categories of Personal Data

- Account metadata: name, email, billing address
- Usage telemetry: project names, run counts, timestamps
- Content the Controller opts to transmit: URLs, screenshots, DOM
  snapshots, request/response bodies captured by the service

## 7. Sub-processors

Processor's current sub-processors are listed and version-controlled at
`docs/compliance/subprocessors.md` (or on Processor's trust page).
Processor will give Controller [30] days' prior notice before adding
or replacing a sub-processor.

## 8. Security Measures

Processor maintains the technical and organisational measures
evidenced in its SOC 2 Type II report and attestations available on
[trust.uxinspect.com](https://trust.uxinspect.com) once published.
Ongoing controls are monitored via the Processor's controls platform
(Vanta or Drata).

## 9. International Transfers

Where Personal Data originates in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, the
parties rely on the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (Module Two,
Controller → Processor) incorporated by reference, plus the UK
International Data Transfer Addendum where the UK GDPR applies.

## 10. Data Subject Rights

Processor will assist Controller with Data Subject requests consistent
with the GDPR, within the commercial terms of the MSA.

## 11. Breach Notification

Processor will notify Controller of a confirmed Personal Data breach
without undue delay, and in any event within [72] hours of becoming
aware.

## 12. Data Deletion / Return

Within [30] days of termination, Processor will return or delete
Personal Data at Controller's election, subject to statutory retention
obligations.

## 13. Audits

Processor will make its most recent SOC 2 Type II report available
under NDA. On-site audits are not contemplated by this Addendum;
additional audits may be agreed in writing subject to reasonable fees.
