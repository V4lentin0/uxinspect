Frequently asked

If your question isn't here, open an issue on GitHub.

Is the free CLI really free forever?

Yes. The core CLI is MIT-licensed. Use it commercially, fork it, ship it inside your own product. The 65+ audits, auto-explore, AI keyless locators, and every reporter stay free.

What runs locally vs in the cloud?

Free and Pro run entirely on your machine. Replay capture writes to .uxinspect/replays/ on disk. Team and Enterprise add a hosted dashboard at app.uxinspect.com so multiple devs see one view across repos.

How does Pro activate without phoning home?

License key checked once via a Cloudflare Worker, signed JWT cached for 30 days. 14-day offline grace if the network is down. Your test runs never leave your machine.

Do I need Team to use the PR bot?

The PR bot, monitor scheduler, hosted dashboard, and alert templates are Team-tier and above. Pro is local-only by design.

What does Cloud Replay add?

A <5KB JS collector you drop into your production HTML. Captures real-user sessions to rrweb, plus heatmaps and funnel analytics. Bolts onto any tier for $49/mo.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly billing, no contracts on Pro or Team. Enterprise is annual with custom terms. Free CLI keeps working forever, regardless.

Is there a startup discount?

Pro is already $19/mo. Team starts at $99 for up to 10 devs. For pre-revenue startups under 5 people, email sales for a 50% Team discount for the first year.

Do framework packs require Pro?

No. Packs are $49 one-time and work with the free MIT core. They ship preset flows + assertions for a specific framework or checkout flow.
